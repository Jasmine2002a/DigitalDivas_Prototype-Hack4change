ABOUT

What is TechPragati

Welcome to TechPragati, your gateway to skill development and economic empowerment. Our platform is dedicated to bridging the growing skills gap driven by rapid technological advancements, with a special focus on marginalized groups, including youth and women in India.


Our Mission

At TechPragati, we believe that access to quality education and training is a fundamental right. Our mission is to provide accessible, affordable, and high-quality training in essential, in-demand skills such as digital literacy, coding, and data analysis. By addressing barriers to education and technology access, we aim to create equitable learning opportunities that enable all individuals to compete effectively in the modern job market.
